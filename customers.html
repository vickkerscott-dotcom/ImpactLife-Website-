Claims Process

How to File a Claim

Filing a claim with Impact Insurance is simple and straightforward. We process most claims within 48 hours and keep you updated throughout the entire process.

1

Report the Incident

Call us or use our online portal to report your claim within 30 days.

2

Submit Documentation

Upload required documents like police reports, medical certificates, or receipts.

3

Claim Assessment

Our team reviews your claim and may contact you for additional information.

4

Payment Processing

Once approved, payment is processed within 7-14 days via your preferred method.

Claim Types We Handle
