Our Story

Founded in 2000

Impact Insurance was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that every African deserves access to reliable financial protection. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we started as a small team with a big vision to transform the insurance landscape across Africa.

What began as a commitment to serve local communities has grown into a comprehensive insurance provider trusted by millions across the continent. Our journey has been guided by innovation, cultural understanding, and an unwavering dedication to our customers.

Our Journey

2000

Company Founded

Impact Insurance established in Nairobi with a mission to provide accessible life insurance across East Africa.

2005

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to West and Southern Africa, introducing micro-insurance products for small businesses.

2010

Digital Transformation

Launched digital self-service platform, making insurance accessible through mobile technology.

2015

Innovation Leadership

Introduced investment-linked insurance products and expanded group insurance solutions.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Rapidly adapted services to support communities during the pandemic with flexible payment options and quick claims processing.

2024

Sustainable Growth

Celebrating 2 million customers and continuing our commitment to innovation and community support across Africa.
